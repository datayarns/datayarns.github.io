Portfolio overview

Data Yarns has a track a record in:

  • Creating digital tools for data collection (such as mobile phones, social media and chatbots) and their application in field experiments.

  • Developing methodological and analytical frameworks which combine emerging data sources (big data and citizen generated data) with traditional survey data to derive enriched insights on social change.

  • Designing research designs that combine quantitative and ethnographic research (observations, focus and individual interviews).

  • Developing approaches for monitoring and evaluation that blend routinely collected administrative information with well-targeted primary data collection.

  • Advancing the discussion and practice around on data ethics, security and privacy through the development of specific solutions.

  • Creating enriched and interactive outputs and products informed by user-centred design principles.
